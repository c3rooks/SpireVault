What signing in does

  • Verifies you actually own the Steam account you're claiming, via Steam OpenID.
  • Mints a 30-day session so you don't have to sign in again.
  • Pulls only your Steam persona name and avatar — never your password.
  • Posts your status to the public feed only while this tab is open and only with what you type.
  • Sign out at any time to drop your row from the feed.
